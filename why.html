Why AI in Education?

Understanding the Importance of AI in Education

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has the potential to revolutionize the education sector by providing personalized learning experiences, automating administrative tasks, and making learning more accessible. With AI, educators can better understand the learning patterns of students, offer tailored resources, and save valuable time by automating repetitive tasks.

Moreover, AI-powered tools help students with disabilities by offering adaptive learning tools such as text-to-speech and speech-to-text technology, enabling a more inclusive learning environment.
