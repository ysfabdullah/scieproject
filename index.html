Why Use AI?

Personalized Learning

AI adapts lessons based on student performance, making education more effective.

Tutoring

AI-powered tutors can assist students with subjects anytime, anywhere.

Efficiency

AI processes information faster than humans, making it useful for tasks like sorting emails, analyzing large datasets.

Advantages and Disadvantages of AI in Education

Advantages

  • Instant feedback for students
  • Accessible learning for all
  • Automated grading saves time
  • Enhances student engagement

Disadvantages

  • Reduced human interaction
  • High implementation costs
  • Bias in AI algorithms
  • Privacy and security concerns
